Snake And Ladder

How to play!

  • Click the dice on the top left corner to roll the dice.
  • The dice gives a random number to the player.
  • The ladder with number implies the ladder will lead the player to the number
  • Clicking on the 'Snake and Ladder' in the game page helps you changing the background color to your preference.
  • The top right corner shows what number you currently are on.
  • Don't try to cheat, as if you change your position by cheating, it will be reverted back.
  • Enjoy the Game!
